This page contains interactive simulations designed to help students visualize complex data structures and their operations.

## Available Visualizers

Click on a topic below to open the interactive visualization tool:

* **[Binary Search Tree (BST)](/files/visualizers/BST.html)** – Visualize insertion, deletion, and tree traversals.
* **[Expression Tree](/files/visualizers/ExpressionTree.html)** – See how infix, prefix, and postfix expressions are represented.
* **[AVL Tree](/files/visualizers/AVL.html)** – Observe self-balancing rotations (LL, RR, LR, RL) in real-time.
